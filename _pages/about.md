## About me

I am a senior research scientist at FANUC advanced research lab. I obtained my Ph.D. at [UC Berkeley](https://www.berkeley.edu) advised by [Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka](http://www.me.berkeley.edu/people/faculty/masayoshi-tomizuka). My research interest lies in the interdisciplinary combination of robotics, learning and control theories. I am interested in building intelligent robots.
